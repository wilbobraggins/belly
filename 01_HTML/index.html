ABOUT ALEXIS

Alexis is a multi-award winning dancer. A passionate, lifelong dancer, Alexis is highly trained in modern, jazz, ballet, and tap, as well as other dance forms such as Flamenco, Tahitian, and Aerial dance.

Trained by the Northwest's most renowned instructors, Alexis performs in a range of venues, from restaurants to nightclubs to bachelorette parties and weddings. As one of the leading bellydancers in Portland, Oregon, Alexis is available to perform at your next party or corporate event in the greater Portland area and beyond.
